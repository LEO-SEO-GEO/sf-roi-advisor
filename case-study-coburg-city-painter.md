## About Coburg City Painters

Coburg City Painters is a professional painting company
operating across Melbourne's inner north — covering Coburg,
Brunswick, Pascoe Vale, Fawkner, Preston, and surrounding
suburbs.

When they came to Salt & Fuessel, they had a real business
with real skills and zero digital presence. No website.
No Google Business Profile. No rankings. No leads coming
from online search.

In Melbourne's inner north — one of the city's most
competitive suburbs for trades businesses — that meant
being invisible to the customers who were already looking
for them.

## The Problem

Coburg City Painters faced three interconnected problems
common to Melbourne trades businesses starting their
digital journey:

**No online credibility**
Without a website or Google presence, potential customers
searching for a painter in Coburg had no way to find them,
verify their work quality, or contact them online.
In a market where 93% of Melbourne consumers check online
before hiring a tradie, no digital presence meant no leads.

**Highly competitive local market**
Melbourne's inner north has a dense concentration of
painting businesses — many with established websites
and years of Google authority built up. Competing as
a new digital entrant required a smarter strategy,
not just a bigger budget.

**Needed results quickly**
A startup trades business cannot wait 18 months for
SEO to compound. Revenue needed to start flowing while
the long-term digital foundation was being built.

## What Salt & Fuessel Did

### Website Development — Built to Convert

The first step was building a website with conversion
as the primary objective — not aesthetics.

Most trades websites are built to look professional.
The Coburg City Painters website was built to turn
visitors into phone calls and enquiries.

**What conversion-focused design looks like for trades:**
- Phone number prominent and click-to-call on every page
- Clear service descriptions matched to what people
  actually search for
- Before and after project photography — the single most
  persuasive content type for painting businesses
- Simple contact form with minimal required fields
- Trust signals above the fold — reviews, years of
  experience, service areas clearly listed
- Fast load time on mobile — most trades searches
  happen on a phone

**The result:** 10% conversion rate.

The Australian average website conversion rate is 1.78%.
The top 20% of websites achieve 3–5%.
10% is exceptional — and it was achieved through deliberate
conversion-focused design, not luck.

---

### Google Business Profile Optimisation

Before running a single paid ad, the Google Business
Profile was built and optimised completely.

For any Melbourne local trades business, Google Business
Profile is the highest ROI marketing asset available —
and it is free.

**What a complete trades Google Business Profile looks like:**
- All services listed with individual descriptions
- Service area suburbs listed specifically — not just
  "Melbourne"
- 20+ genuine Google reviews targeting 4.5+ star rating
- Photos of actual work — before and after for every
  major job type
- Business hours correct and updated for public holidays
- Posts updated regularly with completed projects
  and seasonal offers
- Questions answered promptly

**Why this matters for search:**
Google Maps results for "painters near me" and
"painter Coburg" are dominated by businesses with
complete, active Google Business Profiles and strong
review counts. Getting into the top three Maps results
— the "local pack" — is often more valuable than
ranking on page one of regular search results because
most mobile users click the Maps results first.

### Local SEO — Suburb-Specific Strategy

The SEO strategy was built around a fundamental insight
about how Melbourne residents search for trades businesses:

**People search for tradies in their suburb — not their city.**

Nobody searches "painter Melbourne" when they need their
house painted. They search "painter Coburg", "painter
Brunswick", "painter Pascoe Vale."

The SEO strategy reflected this reality with suburb-specific
pages targeting every area Coburg City Painters services:

**Target keyword structure:**
- "Painters Coburg" / "painting Coburg"
- "Painters Brunswick" / "house painters Brunswick"
- "Painters Pascoe Vale" / "interior painters Pascoe Vale"
- "House painting Preston"
- "Commercial painters Melbourne inner north"

Each suburb page was written with genuine local content —
not duplicated template text with the suburb name swapped.
Google identifies and penalises thin duplicate local pages.
These pages were built to rank and built to convert.

**On-page SEO across the site:**
- Title tags and meta descriptions optimised for
  local search intent
- Heading structure rebuilt to signal service and
  location relevance
- Internal linking structure connecting suburb pages
  to the main service pages
- Site speed and technical performance improved
  to support ranking ability

---

### SEM — Immediate Visibility While SEO Builds

While the SEO foundation was being established —
a process that takes 3–6 months for early traction —
Google Ads provided immediate visibility for
high-intent searches.

Campaigns were structured around:
- Emergency and urgent painting searches
- Suburb-specific painting keywords
- Specific service types (interior painting,
  exterior painting, commercial painting)

Each ad group had its own dedicated landing page
matching the specific service and suburb being targeted —
not a generic homepage.

This structure — specific ad to specific landing page —
is the difference between a 2–3% conversion rate
and the 10% conversion rate Coburg City Painters achieved.

## What This Means for Melbourne Trades Businesses

The Coburg City Painters result demonstrates four
principles applicable to any Melbourne tradie starting
or improving their digital presence:

**Principle 1: Build the website for conversion,
not for appearance**

The most common mistake Melbourne trades businesses make
is investing in a website that looks professional but
is not built to convert visitors into calls.

Before spending on any traffic — organic or paid — ask:
does my website make it easy for a mobile visitor to
contact me within 10 seconds of landing?

If the answer is no, that is where the investment should go.

**Principle 2: Suburb-specific content beats
generic Melbourne content**

"Painters Melbourne" is competitive, expensive, and
attracts broad unqualified traffic.
"Painters Coburg" is specific, lower competition,
and attracts people who actually need a painter
in the suburb you service.

Every Melbourne trades business should have individual
pages for every suburb they service — not one generic
city-wide service page.

**Principle 3: Google Business Profile before
paid advertising**

For Melbourne local trades, Google Business Profile
drives more high-intent leads per dollar than any
paid channel. It is free. Set it up completely before
spending on Google Ads or Meta Ads.

**Principle 4: SEO and Google Ads work better together**

Google Ads provided immediate leads while SEO was
building. SEO data informed which keywords converted
best in paid campaigns. Each channel made the other
more effective.

This combination — immediate results from Ads,
compounding growth from SEO — is the most consistently
effective strategy for Melbourne trades businesses
across every category.
