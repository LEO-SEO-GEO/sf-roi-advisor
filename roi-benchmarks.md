## SEO — Realistic Timeline for Melbourne SMEs

The most common mistake is expecting SEO to work like Google Ads. It doesn't. But the long-term return is significantly higher.

**Months 1–2: Foundation**
Technical fixes, keyword strategy, on-page optimisation. No visible traffic movement yet. This phase is essential — skipping it means slower results later.

**Months 2–4: Early Traction**
First keyword movements appear. Low-to-mid difficulty terms start ranking. 10–20% traffic lift from baseline begins.

**Months 4–6: Conversion Lift**
Traffic grows meaningfully. Early ROI becomes visible. This is when most businesses start to see the investment paying off.

**Months 6–18: Compounding Growth**
Rankings compound. 40–80% overall organic traffic increase is realistic for well-executed campaigns. ROI becomes clearly positive.

**18 months+: Dominance Phase**
Full campaign potential realised. Rankings become difficult for competitors to displace. This is where 8x–15x ROI is achieved.

**Competitive industry note:**
Legal, finance, real estate, and medical in Melbourne require 12–18 months minimum before meaningful first-page visibility. These industries need $3,500–$6,000/month investment to compete.

## Email Marketing — The Most Underused Channel

Email consistently outperforms every other digital channel on pure ROI — yet it's the one Melbourne SMEs invest in least.

- Average return: $36–$42 for every $1 spent
- Top performers: $68 return per $1 spent
- Automated emails earn 16x more per send than manual campaigns
- Abandoned cart emails: 3.33% conversion rate (top performers: 7.69%)
- Effective minimum budget: $500/month

**The catch:** Email only works if you have a list. Building that list is where the investment starts.

---

## Meta Ads — What Melbourne Businesses Actually Pay

Australia is the 3rd most expensive country for Meta advertising — behind only the USA and Canada.

- CPM (cost per 1,000 impressions): $11.04 AUD
- CPC: $0.85 AUD average
- ROAS: 2.2x–5x (retargeting performs better at 3.61x)
- Finance and legal sectors: $15+ CPM
- Minimum effective budget: $1,000/month

Meta works best for brand awareness, retargeting warm audiences, and e-commerce with strong creative. It is not a reliable primary lead generation channel for most Melbourne service businesses.

## GEO — The Emerging Channel Worth Watching

GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) is the practice of getting your business recommended by AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI.

**Why it matters now:**
- Google AI Overviews appear on 48–50% of tracked queries (Feb 2026)
- AI-referred traffic converts at 14.2% vs 2.8% for traditional organic search
- That's 406% better conversion from AI-sourced visitors
- Brands cited in AI Overviews earn 35% more organic clicks than non-cited competitors
- One third of Australian consumers now use AI for shopping decisions — 
  up 45% since 2024

**Early ROI data:**
- Agencies implementing GEO report $3.71 returned per $1 spent
- Timeline: 3–6 months for meaningful AI citation results
- 6–12 months to build sustained AI visibility

| SEO | 30% | $900 |

**The 70/20/10 rule:**
- 70% on proven channels delivering consistent ROI
- 20% on growth channels being scaled

**What not to do:**
Spreading $3,000 across 6 channels means no channel has enough budget to work properly. Concentration beats diversification at SME budget levels.
