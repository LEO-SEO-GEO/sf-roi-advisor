# Melbourne Digital Marketing Decision Tree

A step-by-step guide to finding the right digital marketing service
for your Melbourne business. Answer each question honestly —
the recommendation at the end is based on what actually works,
not what is most profitable to sell.

---

## How to Use This

Work through each step in order. Your answers narrow down the recommendation.
If you are using the AI advisor, it will guide you through these questions
one at a time in plain conversation.

Every recommendation includes a real case study from a Melbourne or
Australian business in a similar situation — with actual numbers.

## Step 3 — What Is Your Current Situation?

**1 — Starting fresh**
No real online presence. No website or a very basic one.
Not showing up on Google at all.

**2 — Have a website but no traffic**
Website exists but gets very few visitors. Not ranking on Google.
No paid advertising running.

**3 — Getting traffic but nothing is converting**
People visit the website but do not call, enquire, or buy.
Traffic exists but leads do not.
*This is the most common — and most fixable — problem.*

**4 — Getting leads but ROI feels low**
Leads and sales are coming in but the cost feels too high
relative to what you are making. Campaigns feel inefficient.

**5 — Running ads but they stopped working**
Google Ads or Meta Ads were working previously but performance
has declined. Results dropped without an obvious reason.
*Usually caused by increased competition, tracking issues,
or campaign structure problems — not the platform itself.*

**6 — Invisible to AI tools**
Competitors are showing up when customers ask ChatGPT or
Google AI for recommendations. Your business is not being mentioned.
*49% of Australians used generative AI in the past 12 months.
One third now use it for shopping decisions.*

## Step 5 — What Is Your Monthly Marketing Budget?

Budget determines what is actually possible. Be honest here —
a mismatched budget and strategy wastes money faster than
doing nothing at all.

**Tier 1 — Under $1,500/month**
Limited options. Must concentrate on one channel only.
Best use: Google Business Profile optimisation (free) +
basic local SEO ($1,000–$1,500/month).
Do not run Google Ads at this budget in competitive Melbourne
categories — insufficient spend for meaningful volume.

**Tier 2 — $1,500–$3,000/month**
Enough for one strong channel or two modest ones.
Best use: Google Ads ($1,000–$1,500 ad spend +
$500–$800 management) OR local SEO.
Not both at full strength yet.

**Tier 3 — $3,000–$5,000/month**
The sweet spot for most Melbourne SMEs.
Can run SEO and Google Ads simultaneously — the most
consistently effective combination.
Jim's Mobile Tyres achieved 39x ROI at this budget tier.

**Tier 4 — $5,000+/month**
Full multi-channel strategy is viable.
Can include GEO, email marketing, social media, and
SEO + Google Ads running simultaneously.
Swann Insurance achieved 13x ROI and 76% conversion
increase at this budget tier.

## Step 6 — Have You Tried Digital Marketing Before?

**Yes, and it worked well**
Note which channel delivered. Double down on it before
adding anything new. The biggest mistake after a working
channel is abandoning it to try something different.

**Yes, but it did not deliver results**
The cause is almost always one of three things:
- Wrong channel for your industry or sales cycle
- Insufficient budget for the competition level in your category
- Poor campaign setup or management — not the platform's fault

Before trying again, identify which of these three caused
the failure. Otherwise you risk repeating it.

**No, this is the first time**
Start with Google Ads for fast feedback and immediate leads.
Add SEO at month 2–3 once ads are proven.
Do not spread budget across multiple channels until you have
proof of what converts for your specific business.

## Recommendation Matrix

### A — Trades & Home Services

**Starting fresh or no traffic + need leads quickly**
*(Situation 1 or 2, Goal B)*

Start with Google Ads. Melbourne trades searches are high-intent —
someone searching "emergency plumber Fitzroy" needs help within
the hour. Google Ads puts you in front of them immediately.

*Minimum budget:* $1,500/month ($1,000 ad spend + $500 management)
*Timeline to first leads:* 1–3 weeks

**Proof:** Eastside Plumbing restructured their Google Ads account
and generated record months of inbound phone enquiries.
The fix was not more spend — it was removing duplicate campaigns
and restructuring by service type.

---

**Getting traffic but not converting**
*(Situation 3)*

Stop spending more on traffic. Fix the website first.
A 10% conversion rate is achievable for Melbourne trades —
Coburg City Painters achieved exactly this alongside a #1 Google
ranking for "Coburg Painters."

Audit your website for these conversion killers:
- No click-to-call button on mobile
- Contact form with too many required fields
- No suburb-specific content matching what visitors searched
- Page load time over 3 seconds

Fix these before increasing a single dollar of ad spend.

---

**Want long-term growth**
*(Goal E, Tier 2–3 budget)*

Combine SEO with Google Ads. SEO for suburb-specific keywords
("painter Brunswick", "plumber Sunshine") compounds over
12–18 months. Google Ads covers you while SEO builds.

Jim's Mobile Tyres used this exact combination —
39x ROI and 124% non-branded traffic growth. The key was
building individual location pages for every suburb served,
not one generic "Melbourne" page.

*Recommended budget:* $2,500–$3,500/month

**Melbourne trades insight:**
Northern and western suburbs (Sunshine, Craigieburn, Werribee,
Tarneit, Melton) have significantly lower competition than inner
suburbs. Entry now builds authority that becomes very difficult
to displace in 2–3 years.

## Red Flags — Fix These Before You Spend Anything

**Website loads in over 3 seconds?**
Fix it first. Every additional second of load time reduces
conversions by 7%. A website loading in 4 seconds loses
20%+ of potential leads before they read a single word.

**Website broken on mobile?**
46% of Melbourne searches happen on mobile. Mobile converts
at 2.9% vs desktop at 4.8%. A broken mobile experience means
you are paying for clicks that will never convert.

**No Google Business Profile?**
This is free and should be done before spending on anything
else. For any Melbourne local business, it is the single
highest ROI marketing action available.

**Fewer than 20 Google reviews?**
67% of consumers will not contact a business with few reviews.
Build your review base before running paid campaigns.
Every new review drives approximately 600 additional searches.

**Running ads to your homepage?**
Ads should go to a dedicated landing page for each service —
not your homepage. This single change consistently delivers
20–40% more conversions without increasing spend.

**Website last updated over 2 years ago?**
Outdated websites underperform regardless of how much
traffic you drive to them. Update before advertising.

## The One Rule That Applies to Every Melbourne SME

**Concentrate before you diversify.**

A $3,000/month budget split across 5 channels means no channel
has enough to work properly. The same $3,000 focused on
SEO + Google Ads is the combination that consistently delivers
the strongest ROI for Melbourne SME owners.

Jim's Mobile Tyres: 39x ROI — SEO only, concentrated budget.
Leo Cussen: 13x ROI — three channels, each with sufficient budget.
Swann Insurance: 13x ROI — multi-channel, but each channel
given enough to optimise properly.

Start with one or two channels. Prove ROI. Then expand.
