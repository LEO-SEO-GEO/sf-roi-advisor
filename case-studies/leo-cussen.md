# Case Study: Leo Cussen — 13x ROI and 300 Enrolments

**Industry:** Legal Education
**Services:** SEO, Google Ads, Social Media, Website Development
**Location:** Melbourne, Victoria
**Result:** 13x ROI, 300 enrolments (capacity exceeded)

---

## About Leo Cussen

Leo Cussen Centre for Law is one of Australia's most respected
providers of Practical Legal Training — the post-degree qualification
required before law graduates can be admitted to practice.

Based in Melbourne, Leo Cussen competes with university-based PLT
providers and national online programs for the same pool of
law graduates each intake period.

When they came to Salt & Fuessel, the goal was straightforward:
increase enrolments for their Practical Legal Training course
beyond the previous maximum of 239 students.

What happened exceeded everyone's expectations.

---

## The Problem

Leo Cussen had strong brand recognition within the Melbourne legal
community. Their program was well-regarded. Their outcomes were strong.

But digital visibility was not keeping pace with the market.
Law graduates were searching online for PLT options and not
consistently finding Leo Cussen at the top of results.

The additional challenge: even when prospects found them,
the enrolment process itself was creating unnecessary friction
that was costing conversions.

---

## What Salt & Fuessel Did

### Multi-Channel Campaign

A coordinated campaign was launched across four channels
simultaneously — each playing a specific role in the
enrolment journey:

**SEO**
Optimised for high-intent keywords relevant to law graduates
researching PLT options in Melbourne and nationally.
Built organic visibility for searches like "practical legal
training Melbourne" and "PLT course Australia."

**Google Ads**
Targeted decision-ready graduates actively searching for
PLT providers. Ads directed to dedicated landing pages —
not the homepage — with a single clear call to action.

**Social Media (Facebook and LinkedIn)**
Facebook reached graduating law students and recent graduates.
LinkedIn targeted the legal professional community — including
law firm partners who influence where graduates complete their PLT.

**Website Development**
The most impactful single change of the entire campaign.

---

### The Enrolment Form Redesign

Before increasing spend on any channel, Salt & Fuessel audited
the conversion path — the journey from interested prospect
to submitted enrolment.

The enrolment form was identified as the primary barrier.

**What was wrong:**
- Too many fields required at the initial expression of interest
- Poor mobile optimisation — significant drop-off on mobile devices
- No progress indicator on multi-step sections
- No instant confirmation after submission
- The form felt like a commitment before the prospect was ready

**What was changed:**
- Reduced required fields at inquiry stage to essentials only
- Rebuilt for mobile — large tap targets, simplified layout
- Added instant automated confirmation with clear next steps
- Structured as a low-commitment first step, not a full application

**The result of the form change alone:**
84% increase in conversion rate.

This single change — not a bigger ad budget, not more traffic —
delivered the largest single performance improvement of the campaign.

## What This Means for Melbourne Education Providers

The Leo Cussen result contains two lessons that apply to every
Melbourne education provider:

**Lesson 1: The conversion path matters as much as the channel**

Most education providers focus entirely on driving more traffic —
more ad spend, more SEO, more social media. The Leo Cussen result
shows that fixing how you convert existing traffic often delivers
greater ROI than buying more of it.

Before increasing any marketing budget, audit your enrolment
or inquiry form. Ask:
- How many fields are required before a prospect can express interest?
- Does it work properly on mobile?
- Does the prospect receive an instant confirmation?
- Does it feel like a low-commitment first step or a full commitment?

**Lesson 2: Multi-channel campaigns outperform single-channel**

SEO built long-term organic visibility.
Google Ads captured decision-ready searchers immediately.
Facebook reached graduates at the awareness stage.
LinkedIn influenced the referral network of legal professionals.

Each channel played a different role in the same enrolment journey.
None of them alone would have delivered the same result.

---

## Relevance for Other Melbourne Businesses

The Leo Cussen methodology applies beyond education.

Any Melbourne business with a contact form, booking form,
or application process should audit that form before
spending more on driving traffic to it.

The question to ask: what percentage of people who reach
your form actually complete it?

If the answer is below 30%, fixing the form will deliver
more ROI than any additional advertising spend.

**Industries where this lesson is most applicable:**
- Health and allied health (booking forms)
- Professional services (inquiry forms)
- Trades (quote request forms)
- Hospitality (reservation and function inquiry forms)
- Any business running Google Ads to a contact page
