# Salt&Fuessel Melbourne Digital Marketing ROI Advisor
A free, open-source decision tree advisor that helps Melbourne and Australian SME owners identify exactly which digital marketing service will deliver the best ROI for their specific business situation.
Built and maintained by [Salt & Fuessel](https://saltandfuessel.com.au) — a Melbourne digital agency based in South Yarra.
---

## What This Does

Most Melbourne business owners know they need digital marketing. What they don't know is which type — and in what order. The wrong choice wastes months and thousands of dollars.

This advisor walks you through a simple set of questions based on your industry, current situation, and growth goal — then tells you exactly what to invest in and what realistic ROI looks like for your budget.

---

## Who This Is For

Small and medium business owners across Melbourne and Greater Victoria who are:
- Not getting ROI from their current agency or marketing spend
- Unsure whether they need SEO, Google Ads, Social Media, GEO, or Email
- Spending on marketing without a clear strategy
- Starting fresh and want to invest in the right channel first
