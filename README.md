# Salt & Fuessel Melbourne Digital Marketing ROI Advisor

A free AI advisor that helps Melbourne and Australian SME owners
find out exactly which digital marketing service will deliver
the best ROI for their business.

Built by [Salt & Fuessel](https://saltandfuessel.com.au) —
a digital agency based in South Yarra, Melbourne.

---

## How to Use This

**Step 1 — Download**
Click the green **Code** button at the top of this page.
Click **Download ZIP.**
Unzip the file on your computer.

**Step 2 — Open Claude.ai**
Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) → Projects →
Create a new project.

**Step 3 — Upload the files**
Inside your new project click **Add Content.**
Upload all the files from the unzipped folder.

**Step 4 — Add the instructions**
Inside the project find **Project Instructions.**
Open the file called `system-prompt.md` from your
unzipped folder, copy everything inside it, and
paste it into the Project Instructions field.
Save.

**Step 5 — Start**
Open a chat inside the project and ask any question
about your marketing. The advisor will take it from there.

## What This Is

Most Melbourne business owners know they need digital marketing.
What they do not know is which type — and in what order.
The wrong choice wastes months and thousands of dollars.

This repo is a structured collection of documents containing:
- A step-by-step decision tree identifying the right marketing
  channel for your specific situation
- Real ROI benchmarks from the Australian market
- Melbourne-specific suburb and competition data
- Industry guides for trades, health, hospitality, retail,
  professional services, and education
- Eight real case studies from Melbourne and Australian
  businesses with actual numbers

When loaded into Claude, these documents turn into a working
advisor that asks you questions and delivers a specific,
evidence-backed recommendation — not generic advice.

## Who This Is For

Small and medium business owners across Melbourne and

- Not getting ROI from their current agency or marketing spend
- Unsure whether they need SEO, Google Ads, Social Media,
  GEO, or Email Marketing
