## The Five-Stage Framework

### Stage 1 — Diagnose Before You Prescribe

Before recommending any channel or campaign, we audit what already exists:

- What is the current conversion rate of the website?
- Where is traffic coming from and what is it doing?
- What have previous campaigns delivered?
- What are competitors doing — and where are the gaps?
- Is the website technically capable of converting paid traffic?

Most wasted marketing spend in Melbourne comes from businesses investing 
in traffic before fixing the destination. Sending 1,000 people to a 
slow, broken, or confusing website is 1,000 wasted opportunities.

**Diagnosis comes before spending.**

---

### Stage 2 — Channel Selection Based on Evidence

Not every channel works for every business. The right channel depends on:

- Industry and competition level
- Geographic focus (inner Melbourne vs growth corridors vs Geelong)
- Sales cycle length (emergency service vs considered purchase)
- Budget available
- Time horizon for results

**The two channels that consistently deliver for Melbourne SMEs:**

**SEO** — builds compounding organic authority over 12–18 months.
Best for businesses investing in long-term growth. The asset keeps 
delivering after the work is done.

**Google Ads** — delivers immediate visibility for high-intent searches.
Best for businesses that need leads now or want to test which keywords 
convert before committing to SEO.

**Combined:** SEO + Google Ads running simultaneously is the highest 
ROI combination for most Melbourne SMEs. Google Ads delivers short-term 
while SEO builds long-term. Each channel informs the other.

---

### Stage 3 — Remove Friction Before Scaling

The most common reason campaigns underperform is not the channel —
it is the conversion path.

We audit and fix:

**Website speed**
Every additional second of load time reduces conversions by 7%.
A website loading in 4 seconds loses 20%+ of potential leads before 
they see a single word.

**Mobile experience**
46% of Melbourne searches happen on mobile. Mobile converts at 2.9% 
vs desktop at 4.8%. Closing that gap is often worth more than 
doubling the ad budget.

**Form and enquiry friction**
Leo Cussen's enrolment form redesign alone delivered an 84% conversion 
rate increase — without changing a single word of ad copy or increasing 
spend. The barrier was the form, not the traffic.

**Trust signals**
93% of Melbourne consumers check reviews before purchasing.
A business with 6 Google reviews and a 3.2-star rating on 
ProductReview.com.au will underperform a competitor with 80 reviews 
and 4.5 stars — regardless of ad spend.

**We fix the destination before scaling the traffic.**

---

### Stage 4 — Test, Measure, and Optimise

Digital marketing is not a set-and-forget investment. Every campaign 
generates data that should be used to improve the next decision.

**What we measure:**
- Cost per lead by channel and campaign
- Conversion rate by landing page
- Return on ad spend by keyword and audience
- Organic traffic growth by keyword cluster
- AI mention rate and sentiment score for GEO campaigns

**How we use it:**
- Pause what is not working — do not let underperforming campaigns 
  drain budget out of loyalty to the original plan
- Double down on what is working — the biggest gains come from 
  concentrating budget on proven performers
- A/B test continuously — small changes to headlines, calls to action, 
  and landing page layouts compound into significant performance lifts

**Swann Insurance example:**
A single landing page banner message change delivered 10% more conversions.
A social media campaign restructure delivered 21.8x return on ad spend.
Neither required additional budget — both required measurement and 
willingness to change what was not working.

---

### Stage 5 — Scale What Works

Once a channel is proven and a conversion path is optimised, scaling 
budget consistently delivers proportional returns.

This is where the 5x–39x ROI results come from — not from finding a 
magic channel, but from methodically fixing problems and concentrating 
investment on proven performers.

**The scaling principle:**
- 70% of budget to proven channels delivering consistent ROI
- 20% to growth channels being scaled
- 10% to emerging channels being tested (currently: GEO)

## Why Most Melbourne SMEs Don't Get ROI

After working with 20+ Melbourne and Australian businesses across 
trades, education, insurance, retail, and professional services, 
the same patterns appear repeatedly:

**Mistake 1 — Spreading budget too thin**
$500/month across 5 channels means no channel has enough budget to 
work. Concentration always outperforms diversification at SME 
budget levels.

**Mistake 2 — Paying for traffic to a broken website**
Traffic is only valuable if the destination converts. Fixing 
conversion issues delivers better ROI than buying more traffic.

**Mistake 3 — Choosing the wrong channel for the sales cycle**
Google Ads works for emergency plumbers. It is expensive and 
inefficient for accountants whose clients take 3 months to decide.
The channel must match how your customers actually buy.

**Mistake 4 — Stopping SEO before it compounds**
SEO takes 3–6 months to show early results and 12–18 months to 
reach peak performance. Most businesses stop at month 4 — 
just before the compounding begins.

**Mistake 5 — Ignoring AI search visibility**
A growing number of Melbourne consumers now ask ChatGPT or 
Google AI for recommendations before visiting a single website.
Businesses not investing in GEO are invisible to this audience.
